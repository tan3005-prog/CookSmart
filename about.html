Our Story

“This app was born from a simple observation: too much good food ends up in the trash, not because it's bad, but because people don't know what to do with it. We've all been there — coming home tired, opening the fridge and feeling overwhelmed by random ingredients that don't seem to go together. We created this platform to bridge that gap between what's in your kitchen and what's on your table. Our vision is a world where home cooking is accessible, creative, and sustainable. Where every carrot, every grain of rice, and every leftover herb finds its way into a delicious meal instead of the garbage bin. ”

-Team CookSmart
